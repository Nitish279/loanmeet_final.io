How it works?

Get started in just four steps

1

Aadhar Submission

You need to provide basic KYC information on our mobile app/website. Just enter your PAN card number, Aadhar card number and upload scan copies of both and you are all set for the next step.

2

Bank Statement

You are a small business and providing us company balance-sheet, Profit-Loss statements seems like a huge burden. No issues, we just require your company bank statement for evaluation. Just upload last 3 month bank statement and done.

3

Approval

No we don’t take forever to process your loan application. After you have provided us KYC information and submitted all the documents mentioned above we will have loan decision within 48 hours and that’s our promise.

4

Disbursal

Once a loan/credit line is approved, we disburse money within 1 working day. We offer flexible repayment options based on nature and size of business. Even after loan disbursal, our team would support you for all future financial assistance.
